Tree Health FAQ

Common questions about keeping your Antelope Valley trees healthy.

How do I know if my tree is sick?

Look for signs like discolored leaves, premature leaf drop, dead branches, or visible fungus/pests. If you're unsure, our health assessment can provide answers.

How often should I fertilize my trees?

Most trees in the AV benefit from deep root fertilization once or twice a year, typically in spring or fall, to support growth and root health.

Can you save a dying tree?

It depends on the cause and severity. Many stress factors can be reversed with proper care, but some advanced diseases may require removal. Early detection is key.
